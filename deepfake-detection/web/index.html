About DeepDetect

DeepDetect uses a multimodal cross-attention architecture that jointly analyzes visual and audio streams to identify deepfake manipulations.

Unlike single-modality detectors, our system exploits the fact that deepfake audio and video are generated independently — leaving subtle cross-modal desynchronization artifacts that are invisible to the human eye but detectable by our model.
